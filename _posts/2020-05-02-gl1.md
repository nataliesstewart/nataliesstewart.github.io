In this incredibly brief post, I would like to just mention a cute fact. (I will
post more once the semester ends.) The J-homomorphism gives us a map
$$\CP^\infty\to \BU \to \BGL_1(\S)$$. The bottom cell $$S^2$$ detects $$\eta\in
\pi_2 \BGL_1(\S) = \pi_1(\S)$$. Because the map $$S^2\to \BGL_1(\S)$$ factors
through $$\CP^2$$, we find that the composite $$S^3\xrightarrow{\eta} S^2\to
\BGL_1(\S)$$ is null. So, $$\eta \cdot \eta = 0$$ in $$\pi_\ast \GL_1(\S)$$.
